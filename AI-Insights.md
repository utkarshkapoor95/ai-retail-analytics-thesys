## 🔍 Q1: Give me profit optimization strategy with data explanation

### 📊 Key Findings:

- Overall Profit Margin: **~11%**
- Total Profit: **~$175K**
- Significant losses identified in specific products
- Strong margin variation across categories and regions

### ❗ Critical Issues:

- Loss-making products identified (some with extremely negative margins)
- Furniture category shows weak profitability
- Central region underperforming compared to West

### 💡 Strategy:

1. **Remove Loss-Making Products**
   - Certain products generate heavy losses → eliminate or reprice

2. **Focus on High-Margin Categories**
   - Technology category shows strong margins → increase investment

3. **Improve Regional Performance**
   - Apply West region strategy to Central region

4. **Optimize Pricing Strategy**
   - Especially in Furniture category

### 📈 Expected Impact:

- +25–30% profit improvement possible  
- Increase margin from ~11% to ~14%+

## 🔍 Q2: Which category is hurting profitability and why?

### 📊 Answer:

👉 **Furniture category is hurting profitability**

### ❗ Why?

- Low or negative profit margins  
- High sales but poor returns  
- Pricing inefficiencies  
- High cost structure  

### 📉 Example:

- Furniture Tables show negative margins  
- Some products generate losses despite high revenue  

### 💡 Recommendation:

- Review pricing strategy  
- Reduce costs or renegotiate suppliers  
- Focus more on profitable categories like Technology
